What is Svel

A personal log for life underwater

Svel is a mobile app for logging your scuba dives, snorkeling trips, and freediving sessions - depth, duration, location, conditions, and photos, all in one place.

Whether you're chasing your first open-water certification or your hundredth reef dive, Svel keeps a map and a record of everywhere you've been.
